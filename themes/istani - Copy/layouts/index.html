What lies hidden... must be found

10 years later, The Chronicles of Spellborn rises from the ashes!

It was November 2008 when the game called The Chronicles of Spellborn was first released. The game is marked as an MMORPG, but stood out from the rest, because of its unique art style and combat system. The game had a troublesome launch, and after being online for just 2 years, the servers shut down for good.

Since then, a group of enthousiasts started a project called Spellborn Reborn. Together they tried reverse engineering the game, and by doing so, create a game server for people to join.

Now, more than 10 years later, the moment has finally come for everybody to play this rough diamond once more. Set foot in the Enclave and amaze yourself once more of the stunning shards that the game offers. Even after 10 years the game still holds up pretty good, and the team even managed to fix some issues in the game.
